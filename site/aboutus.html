Our Journey

We live in a beautiful world. A world masterfully crafted by the intelligent hands of nature. A world full of vibrant colours, intoxicating aromas, blissful sounds and exhilarating flavours. A world for us to discover and cherish. A few years ago, we set out on a journey. A journey to discover the wonders of the world. Our path led us to tea. A beverage which is as enjoyable as it is beneficial. A brew both flavourful and revitalizing, simple and elegant. A brew equally pleasing to all the senses. Truly, a gift of nature. At Seven Essence, we are passionate about sharing the fruits of our labour with the world, with you so that you can appreciate the intelligence of nature, so that you can rediscover our beautiful world, so that you can rediscover yourself. Our teas are as unique as you are. Take out the time to find the ones which call out to you personally. Make it your own. The fact that so much has been written and said about a cup of tea proves there is a whole new world hidden in your tea cup.

“It’s not just a cup of tea. It’s an ode to nature’s intelligence. It is your connection to nature, to yourself. “
